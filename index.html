TALK FOR A MINUTE RULES

Click the first team's name to start the clock. The other team may challenge for Hesitation, Repetition, going Off Topic or Bad English. After a challenge, press PAUSE. If the challenge is successful, subtract the relevant points and press the challenging team's button to continue. The team speaking when the countdown ends get 50 points. Play for as many rounds as there are students. Use Oops to correct mistakes. Happy talking!
